Ms. Fr. 640 contains a number of sketches made by its anonymous author-practitioner to help describe or illustrate the processes and materials of the entries or recipes. Explore these drawings below, from a diagram for preparing a mold of a lizard on [fol. 124v](/folios/124v) and a furnace with bellows on [fol. 16r](/folios/16r) to the texture of dragon‚Äôs blood on [fol. 29v](/folios/29v) and a fountain system to carry water higher than its source on [fol. 80r](/folios/80r).
